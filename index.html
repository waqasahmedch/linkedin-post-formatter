How does this work? (click to expand)

LinkedIn doesn't support real rich text. This tool uses Unicode math characters that look bold/italic everywhere — including LinkedIn. Underline and strikethrough use combining characters. That means it's real text, not an image, and it stays formatted when you paste.

Line Spacer inserts an invisible character so LinkedIn doesn't collapse your blank lines.

Tip: Select text first, then click a formatting button. Or use keyboard shortcuts Ctrl/Cmd+B, Ctrl/Cmd+I, Ctrl/Cmd+U.

Runs 100% offline in your browser. No data leaves your device.
